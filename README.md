### 📌 Important Note

If you are an editor or reviewer of this manuscript, we would like to explicitly clarify that our decision to withhold the full public release of our code and dataset at the current stage is in no way a compromise of academic integrity or transparency. The real-world annotated hospital clinical dataset used in our work is inherently scarce: its collection and professional annotation require rigorous validation by qualified clinicians, entailing enormous time and labor costs. Meanwhile, the model training, testing and iterative optimization involved in this research have taken extensive and painstaking efforts. We solemnly commit that the complete code and full dataset will be fully open-sourced immediately upon the formal acceptance of our manuscript.For other viewers, given the scarcity of the private clinical dataset and the substantial efforts invested in the model development, we will release the full code and dataset to the public once the paper is officially accepted.Thank you for your understanding, and please stay tuned for further updates.
